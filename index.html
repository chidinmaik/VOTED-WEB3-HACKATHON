About

VOTED is designed to provide a secure, transparent, and user-friendly voting experience for all Nigerian citizens. Whether you're a first-time voter or a seasoned participant in the democratic process, our system makes it easy to cast your ballot and have your voice heard.
Create an Account
To participate part in the voting process create an account. Your National ID, birth certificate, and fingerprints will be required in order to verify your identity and eligibility to vote. If you are registered, skip ahead and go right to the voting page!
Cast Your Vote
TAfter you've registered, go to the Voting Page to cast your ballot. Before making your decision, you will be able to view the candidates' profiles.
View Result
Following the voting, you can view the election results on the Results Page.You can also get detailed information on the number of votes cast, the winners, and any other pertinent information.
